The Game

A one-day seminar wargame

Participants manage sustained U.S.–PRC strategic competition with non-military instruments. The Red Team plays the competitor: respond to Blue, pressure or court Green, and advance an alternative system.

Why we play

Purpose

Stress-test U.S. statecraft by giving the competitor real agency: surface the inflection points where Red action fractures the U.S.-led order or hardens it.

Your mandate

Red Team objective

  • Advance the alternative order.Grow the BRICS+ SSSI footprint; entrench PRC standards.
  • Fracture the alliance.Peel partners from Blue through incentive or coercion.
  • Blunt Blue’s instruments.Reduce the bite of sanctions, controls, and restrictions.
  • Manage escalation.Win position without triggering a unifying overreaction.
